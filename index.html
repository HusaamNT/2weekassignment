About

My name is Husaam Naeem Tariq. I am currently a student at the University of Birmingham, learning to become a Full Stack Developer!

Abilities

During the lockdown, I understood that the online world has much more to offer than it once seemed. I decided to change my career to something that suited me. During the second year of my degree, I dropped out to start an online business when the whole world was at home and online. There I decided that pursuing the ability to create online platforms would be the future of this generation and I wanted to become a part of that. I am looking to build experience in problem solving code and building systems. I learned that I am able to adapt to changing situations as well as understanding what I want to do and that I am able to take the right actions to pursue that. I have had part-time jobs in my father's and grandfather's businesses. There, I learnt leadership skills from assisting and overlooking others. I mainly worked as a barister and as a server. Ensuring that the customers were attended to and happy about their service as well as keeping the flow of making a list of hot and cold drinks aswell as taking orders, serving their food on time and asuring them when things got busy or the orders arrived incorrect. Establishing a standard in customer service is a priority in the restauranting business. I learned that even when things go wrong, the attitude you must maintain can decide whether thats a customer lost or gained.
